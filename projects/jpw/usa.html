How do I discover what meteor shower was visible in Northern Europe in August 2000?

Meteor showers are annual events, peaking on pretty much the same date every year. Because of leap years, the peaks may vary by a day or two, but are very reliable. The main meteor shower in August is the Perseids, which peaks on August 12 or 13. I still have my Observer's Handbook from 200, and the Perseid peak that year was on August 12 at 04h UT.
